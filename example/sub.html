Subtitle Generator

This is a subtitle generator. You can customize everything you want yourself, from the text size, to the letter gradient, to the speed of word highlighting.

This generator's advantages lie in its ability to operate without an internet connection and its low resource consumption, which is crucial for both extended battery life and fast subtitle generation.

Stack: TypeScript, Node.js
